About Life Cunningham

Crafting magical worlds and unforgettable characters

The Story Behind the Stories

Hello! I'm Life Cunningham, a fantasy author who believes in the power of storytelling to transport readers to worlds where magic is real and anything is possible.

My journey as a writer began in the quiet corners of libraries and the magical realms of my imagination. Growing up, I was always drawn to stories that blended the ordinary with the extraordinary—tales where characters discovered hidden powers, faced impossible odds, and found strength they never knew they possessed.

The Spirit Caster series was born from my fascination with the idea that magic exists in the spaces between what we see and what we believe. Each character in my stories represents a piece of the human experience—our fears, our hopes, our capacity for growth and transformation.

My Writing Journey

From first draft to published author

2020 - The Beginning

Started writing Winters' War during a particularly challenging time. The story of Elara Winters became my escape and my anchor.

2021 - Finding My Voice

Completed the first draft and began the long process of revision. Learned that writing is rewriting, and every word matters.

2022 - Building Worlds

Developed the magic system and worldbuilding for the Spirit Caster universe. Created maps, character bios, and detailed lore.

2023 - Publication

Winters' War was published, marking the beginning of a new chapter in my life as a published author.

2024 - The Adventure Continues

Working on the second book in the Spirit Caster series and planning future projects that will expand the magical universe.

My Writing Process

How I bring magical worlds to life

Inspiration

Every story begins with a spark—a character, a situation, or a question that won't let go. I collect these moments in notebooks and let them simmer.

Worldbuilding

I create detailed maps, magic systems, and cultural backgrounds. The world must feel real and lived-in before the story can truly come alive.

Character Development

Characters drive the story. I spend time understanding their motivations, fears, and growth arcs before putting pen to paper.

First Draft

The messy, beautiful process of getting the story down. I write quickly, focusing on getting the bones of the story on the page.

Revision

This is where the magic happens. I revise for plot, character development, pacing, and voice. Multiple drafts are essential.

Publication

Sharing the story with readers is both terrifying and exhilarating. Every reader brings something new to the story.

Fun Facts About Me

Get to know the person behind the stories

Coffee Connoisseur

I can't write without a perfect cup of coffee. My desk is always cluttered with mugs and coffee stains on my notebooks.

Nature Lover

I find inspiration in forests, mountains, and starry nights. Many of my magical settings are inspired by real places I've visited.

Music Enthusiast

I create playlists for each book and character. Music helps me get into the right emotional space for writing.

Cat Person

My writing companion is a fluffy cat named Luna who thinks my keyboard is her personal bed.

Voracious Reader

I read 50+ books a year across all genres. You can't be a good writer without being a good reader first.

Night Owl

My best writing happens between 10 PM and 2 AM. There's something magical about writing in the quiet hours.
