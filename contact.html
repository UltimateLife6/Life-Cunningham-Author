Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

When will the next book be released?

I'm currently working on Spirit Caster #2 and aiming for a 2024 release. Join my newsletter to be the first to know when pre-orders are available!

Do you accept fan art or fan fiction?

I absolutely love seeing fan art and creative content inspired by my books! Feel free to tag me on social media or send it via email. I try to share as much as I can!

Can I request a signed book?

I offer signed books through select independent bookstores. Check my social media for announcements about signing events and availability.

Will there be more books in the Spirit Caster series?

The Spirit Caster series is planned as a trilogy, but I have many more stories to tell! I'm already brainstorming ideas for future series and standalone novels.

Do you do school visits or book club appearances?

Yes! I love connecting with readers through virtual and in-person events. Send me an email with details about your event, and I'll do my best to make it work.

How can I support your work?

The best ways to support me are: buy my books (especially from independent bookstores), leave reviews, share my work on social media, and join my newsletter. Every little bit helps!
